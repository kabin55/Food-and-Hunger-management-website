Global Hunger Index (GHI)

The Global Hunger Index (GHI) measures and tracks hunger at the global, regional, and national levels. It combines multiple indicators to provide a comprehensive assessment of hunger and malnutrition. The GHI score indicated the severity of hunger on a scale from 0 to 100.

Interpretation of GHI scores:
  • GHI Score < 10: Low hunger severity
  • GHI Score 10 - 19.9: Moderate hunger severity
  • GHI Score 20 - 34.9: Serious hunger severity
